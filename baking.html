Moms Best Ever Banana Muffins

  • 3 large bananas
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 375˚ F.
  2. Mash bananas. Add sugar, lightly beaten egg and oil and mix until well-combined.
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  4. Spoon mixture into greased muffin pan and bake for about 20 minutes.

Vanilla Scones

Scones

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, cubed

Vanilla Glaze

  • 1 1/2 cups icing sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water (you may need nore)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 425˚ F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixer, fitted with a paddle attachment, mix together the flour, salt, baking powder and sugar. Add cold cubed butter and mix on low speed until coarse crumbs form - be careful not to overmix.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg, milk and vanilla, then add it to the mixer and mix until dough comes together. It will be crumbly.
  4. Flour a clean surface and knead dough just a bit until it comes together. Form it into a circle about 1" thick. use a knife to cut scones into 8 wedges. Place on a baking skeet and sprinkle with sugar.
  5. Bake 10-14 minutes or until edges are slightly golden. Remove from oven and place on a cooling rack.
  6. While scones are cooling, add all the glaze ingredients into a bowl and whisk - glaze will be slightly thick but should drizzle off a spoon. Use a spoon to spread the glaze over cooled scones. Let stand until glaze is set.

Granola

  • 2 cups large flake oats
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  1. Preheat oven to 325˚ F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix oats, cinnamon and salt.
  3. Mix honey, peanut butter and vanilla over medium heat on the stove.
  4. Stir both mixtures together.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, stirring halfway. Let sit on baking sheet until cooled.
